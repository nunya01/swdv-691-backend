About the DIY Tool Exchange

This site is a place for DIY'ers (or anyone else!) to loan and borrow tools.

Currently, the site is like a "lending library" for tools.

In order to facilitate a larger library, in the future, tool owners may be able to charge a small rental fee.

Some of the tools on this site are FSBO.
